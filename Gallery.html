About

A well-respected freelance musician based in London, Henry James has worked with world renowned songwriters, artists and producers and has played in venues, festivals and recording studios around the globe. Henry is now making a bold entrance into the artistic world, revealing an honest, direct yet sophisticated approach to songwriting, finessed in a unique style that's rich in diversity; steeped in funk, jazz, rock and pop.
